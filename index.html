Privacy Policy for VerbChecker

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

VerbChecker ("we," "us," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection when you use the VerbChecker mobile application ("App").

VerbChecker is a Dutch verb conjugation tool designed to help language learners master Dutch grammar. The App operates entirely offline and does not require any personal information to function.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

VerbChecker does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App does not require:

  • Your name or email address
  • Phone number or device identifier
  • Location data
  • Age or demographic information
  • Any account or login credentials

2.2 Usage Data

VerbChecker does NOT collect any usage analytics, crash reports, or behavioral data. We do not track:

  • Which verbs you search for
  • How often you use the App
  • Session duration or frequency
  • Device information or app version
  • Crash reports or performance metrics

2.3 Automatic Data Collection

The App does not use:

  • Cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Web beacons or pixels
  • Third-party analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • Advertising networks or user profiling

3. How We Use Information

Since VerbChecker collects no personal or usage data, we have no information to use. The App simply provides Dutch verb conjugation functionality offline without any data transmission.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

The only data stored on your device is the embedded verb database (verbs.json) necessary for the App to function. This file:

  • Contains only Dutch verb conjugation data
  • Is stored locally within the App's sandbox
  • Is not transmitted to any external servers
  • Cannot be accessed by other applications

4.2 Internet Connectivity

VerbChecker operates completely offline. The App:

  • Does not require an internet connection
  • Does not transmit any data to servers
  • Does not connect to cloud services
  • Works entirely on your device

4.3 Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is minimal security risk. Your device's built-in security measures protect the App's data.

5. Third-Party Services

VerbChecker does not integrate with any third-party services, libraries, or APIs that collect user data. The App is completely self-contained and independent.

We do not use:

  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media SDKs
  • Cloud storage services
  • Crash reporting tools

6. Children's Privacy

VerbChecker is suitable for all ages, including children under 13. Since the App:

  • Collects no personal information
  • Does not require user accounts
  • Contains no advertisements or external content
  • Does not transmit any data

The App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar privacy regulations for children worldwide.

7. Permissions

VerbChecker requires minimal device permissions:

  • Internet Permission: Not required (App is offline)
  • Location Permission: Not required
  • Camera/Microphone: Not required
  • Contacts/Calendar: Not required
  • Storage: Limited to App-specific directory only

8. Your Rights

Since VerbChecker collects no personal data, you have no data to request, access, modify, or delete. Your privacy is protected by design.
